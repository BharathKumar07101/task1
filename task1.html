chicken fired rice

ingredients:

  • 1 cup of cooked rice
  • 100g of chicken
  • 1 egg
  • 1 onion
  • 2 spoons of oil
  • 1 spoon of chill powder
  • 1 spoon tasting salt
  • 1 spoon of soya sauce
  • 1/4 turmeric powder

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan
  2. Add chicken and cook until it turns golden brown
  3. Add chopped onion
  4. Add chill powder and turmeric powder
  5. Push ingredients to one side and scramble the eggs
  6. Add cooked rice and mix everything together
  7. Add soy sauce and salt well
  8. Cook for 2–3 minutes and serve hot

Kabaddi is a contact team sport played between two teams of seven players. It is one of the traditional games of South Asia. In the game, a raider enters the opposing half of the court to tag the defenders and attempt to return within 30th seconds without being tackled. Players need proper oxygen like H2O during the game. Kabaddi is governed by the IKF.
